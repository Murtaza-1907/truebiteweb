TrueScore Framework

How every best pick is selected.

TrueBite compares products inside the same category so the score is fair, practical and easy to understand before checkout.

01

Nutrition balance

Products score higher for useful protein, fibre and sensible calories. Heavy sugar, excess salt and weak macro balance reduce the score.

02

Ingredient quality

Cleaner ingredient lists are preferred. Products lose points when the label depends on avoidable additives, vague blends or heavy processing.

03

Category fairness

A chocolate is not judged like oats. Every product is compared against two close competitors from the same Indian grocery category.

04

Value for money

The final pick must still make sense for everyday buying. Price, pack usefulness and nutrition-per-rupee are included before a winner is shown.

What the score considers

35%Nutrients: protein, fibre, sugar, salt, calories and fat balance.
30%Ingredients: simplicity, recognisable foods and additive load.
20%Category fit: how well the product performs against direct alternatives.
15%Price value: whether the healthier choice is still practical to buy.
